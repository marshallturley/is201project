Bio

José Ramírez is a Dominican professional baseball player and the longtime third baseman for the Cleveland Guardians. Known for his switch-hitting power, speed, and elite defense, Ramírez has established himself as one of the most complete players in Major League Baseball. A multiple-time All-Star and Silver Slugger winner, he is respected not only for his on-field performance but also for his leadership and loyalty to Cleveland, where he has spent his entire MLB career.
